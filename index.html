FACILITIES

Joel & Diane Bloom Wellness and Events Center (WEC)

The Joel & Diane Bloom Wellness and Events Center (WEC) is a holistic hub offering wellness programs, community events, and transformative experiences to promote health and well-being.

The Naimoli Family Athletic and Recreational Facility

The Naimoli Family Athletic and Recreational Facility is a state-of-the-art center that provides diverse athletic programs, fitness opportunities, and recreational activities for all ages.

Warren Street Fitness Center

The Warren Street Fitness Center is a comprehensive facility equipped with modern workout amenities and classes, designed to support health and fitness goals for individuals and the community.

Lubetkin Field at Mal Simon Stadium

Lubetkin Field at Mal Simon Stadium is a premier athletic venue featuring a turf field designed for football and soccer, hosting a variety of sports events and community activities.

Branch Brook Park

Branch Brook Park is a scenic urban park known for its beautiful cherry blossom trees, walking trails, and recreational facilities, offering a peaceful escape for visitors year-round.

The Sandy & John Olson Fitness Center

The Sandy & John Olson Fitness Center is a modern facility that offers a wide range of fitness equipment, classes, and programs aimed at promoting health and wellness for all members of the community.
